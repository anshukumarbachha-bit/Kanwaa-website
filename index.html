Our Science-Backed Process

Most water brands stop at basic filtration. At Kanwaa, we go further. Our water undergoes Ozonisation, a process far superior to standard chlorination, ensuring that even the most microscopic bacteria and viruses are neutralized without leaving a chemical aftertaste.

We also utilize Reverse Osmosis (RO) at a precision level to balance the Total Dissolved Solids (TDS). This ensures the water is light on your digestive system while retaining the essential minerals your body needs to stay hydrated and energetic.

Commitment to Hygiene

Trust is built in the details. Our 20-Litre jars go through a multi-stage automated washing and sterilization process before refilling. We use food-grade, BPA-free materials for all our packaging to ensure that no harmful chemicals leach into your water during storage or transit.

From the moment the water is sourced to the final seal on the bottle, there is zero human contact. This "Hands-Free" philosophy is what makes Kanwaa the gold standard for families and corporate offices alike.
